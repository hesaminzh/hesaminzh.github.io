About

I hold an M.Sc. in Telecommunications Engineering from Shahid Beheshti University (National University of Iran). My research focuses on signal processing and optimization for wireless communications, particularly in reconfigurable intelligent surface (RIS), beyond-diagonal RIS (BD-RIS), integrated sensing and communication (ISAC), and backscatter communication (BackCom). I am passionate about developing and analyzing advanced methods to enhance the performance and efficiency of next-generation wireless systems.
