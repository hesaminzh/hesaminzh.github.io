About

I hold an M.Sc. in Telecommunications Engineering from Shahid Beheshti University (National University of Iran). My research focuses on signal processing and optimization for wireless communications, particularly in millimeter-wave (mmWave) systems, reconfigurable intelligent surfaces (RIS), beyond-diagonal RIS (BD-RIS), and integrated sensing and communications (ISAC). I am passionate about developing and analyzing advanced methods to enhance the performance and efficiency of next-generation wireless systems.
